お部屋の紹介

Unique 3 story house with roof deck, all modern facilities but a Japanese style through out. There are two bathrooms, so you can have your own toilet. A Japanese style bath is available that is large enough for two people.

You have a private bedroom and private balcony to relax on. There is a wifi system throughout the house. If you need a portable wifi while staying with us for 2 or more days we can provide for a fee.

You can have access to laundry facilities for a nominal fee. Somebody will be here to help you with any questions you have. as well as suggestions for sightseeing nearby.

The area around our place is still busy as all Japan is but generally quite quiet especially on the weekend. Our train station is about 10 minute walk along the river. From there Osaka is about 15 minutes, Kobe about 25 minutes, Kyoto and Nara are about 1 hour.

As mentioned before there is a river nearby with a 10km jogging route. We are also 2km away from the world famous Hanshin Tigers Baseball stadium.
There are 2 Convenience stores within 5 minutes walk that are open 24 hours. Being a residential area there aren't many restaurants nearby but everything is a short train ride away.

Last but not least we have a dog named Nico, she is a Shiba, which is a traditional Japanese breed. She is very people friendly, so a bonus for dog lovers. She is very relaxed and never barks so she will not disturb you.

You pretty well have access to the whole house, including the roof top deck if the weather is appropriate.

We also live here but we respect the privacy of the guests and are usually home in the evenings for any kind of questions our guests might have.

We live in a residential area so there aren't many restaurants or bars in our area. However, close to Osaka or Kobe.

It is about a 10 minute walk to our station. The name of the station is Mukogawa and it is on a private train line called Hanshin. From there it is easy to get to Osaka or Kobe.
